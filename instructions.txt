Fractal Cube World:
- You are in a recursive world of cubes.
- Each block can be "Split" by clicking it.
- Once split, click it again to "Shrink" and enter it.
- The world inside is a 16x16x16 grid of more cubes.
- Walls are solid and perfectly aligned to prevent gaps.
- Use WASD on desktop to move, and the joystick on mobile.
